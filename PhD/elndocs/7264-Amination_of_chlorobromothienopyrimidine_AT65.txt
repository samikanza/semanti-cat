Amination of chlorobromothienopyrimidine (AT-6-5)
1st April 2013 @ 23:30


Synthesis for more substrate.


The reaction was performed four times, each with 0.300 g starting material (AT-5) in 8 mL NH3/isopropanol (1:1). The crude was combined and purified by flash column chromatography (95:4.5:0.5 dichloromethane:methanol:ammonia) to give the product as a white solid.
